What you just watched is a scripted demo — it shows the shape of the work. The real ghost team runs on Claude Code: it reads your actual codebase, talks to real users through your connected tools, and writes real, shippable code. Same seven teammates — actually doing the job. Pull just this folder (no repo baggage):

# grab the team — just the ghost-team folder, nothing else
npx degit chiibitsu/labs/ghost-team my-ghost-team
cd my-ghost-team && ./install.sh

# then open Claude Code in your project and type:
/ship a tool that turns voice memos into meeting notes
