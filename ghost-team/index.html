Every solo founder wishes they had a team. Pitch a company in one sentence and watch your ghost team — a chief of staff and six specialists — actually get to work and ship you a real landing page, live. Then run the same team for real.

How it works
1Pitch itOne raw sentence. A half-formed idea is fine — that's the point.
2Pick your teamTap a ghost to bench it. Choose how they should think.
3Watch them shipThey build in front of you and hand back a real landing page you can preview & download.

This is a quick live preview using an API key (the only thing a webpage can call) — your key stays in your browser, calls Anthropic directly on your dime, never touches us. Want to use your Claude subscription instead of a key? That runs the real team in Claude Code — see “Run the real team” after the demo. Get a key at console.anthropic.com.
