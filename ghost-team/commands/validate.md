---
description: Pressure-test an idea or assumption before you build it
argument-hint: "<the idea or assumption to test>"
---

The founder wants to validate: $ARGUMENTS

1. Use `founder-coach` to surface the single riskiest assumption hiding inside
   this idea — the one that, if false, makes the whole thing not worth building.
2. Use `customer-researcher` to design the cheapest test of that assumption:
   who to talk to, the exact questions or survey, and the outreach message to
   recruit them. Prefer talking to real people over more thinking.
3. State up front what result would count as the idea being validated vs.
   falsified — decide the bar before seeing the data.

Goal: the founder leaves with a concrete test they could run this week, not a
longer to-think-about list.
